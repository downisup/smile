About us

smile recognizes the need to support and encourage the sustainable development of education for the children and adolescents, empowerment of women and the environment for self-sustaining entities. Mission: Every Girl in School aims to support the education of girls across the country - and you can be a part of it. Just a small donation from you every month can make a huge difference to one girl's life. The path to recovery can be long and arduous,but you can make a difference by joining the rebuilding efforts.we are here to raise money to help the homeless,to find a cure for disease,to help natural disasters victims.. Be a part of the breakthrough and make someone’s dream come true. Your contribution can help achieve a solution. Give your share to show you care.
